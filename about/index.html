About Me

Hey, I’m Drake — part product lead, part hands-on builder, and always exploring. From remote teams to blockchain startups, I’ve spent the last decade learning how great software gets made (and how to avoid burning out more than just servers).

Mastery isn’t the end of learning; it’s the start of asking better questions.

My curiosity didn’t stop at product roadmaps — it pulled me into code, teams, and everything in between. I’ve built prototypes, shipped experiments, and helped scale ideas from startup scrappiness to strategic execution. Now, stepping into senior leadership, I bring both a ground-level understanding and a high-level perspective to the work I do.

I’ve learned that the best products—and the best ideas—start with curiosity and a willingness to tinker.

Gallery / Unsplash

I started my career in AAA gaming at Electronic Arts, where I got a front-row seat to how great teams build massive, immersive digital products. That background taught me the value of storytelling, systems, and iteration — lessons I carried into ecommerce and fintech. Payment integrations eventually introduced me to crypto, and from there, I dove headfirst into blockchain product development. Today, I bring that same spirit of exploration and structure to every product I touch.

When I'm not building products or tinkering with code, you’ll probably find me in a cozy kitchen experimenting with recipes I’ve picked up while traveling the world, gaming late into the night, or exploring a new city with a laptop in my backpack. Whether it’s food, travel, or side hustles, I’m always chasing that mix of creativity, challenge, and a bit of fun.

The Brains Behind the Build

Building this blog is my way of bringing together everything I’m passionate about — from product management and coding to the journeys that come with working remotely around the world. It’s a space where I share lessons learned, practical insights, and sometimes a few recipes or travel stories along the way. I want this to be a resource for anyone navigating the intersection of technology, creativity, and curiosity. The tech world can feel complex, but through this blog, I aim to make it approachable, interesting, and inspiring for everyone.

Photo by Pablo Stanley on Lummi

Looking back, each step in my journey—from launching my first game project at Electronic Arts (NCAA Basketball 10) to diving into ecommerce payments and exploring blockchain—has shaped how I approach product and tech today.

As someone who bridges product and code, I’m always exploring new skills and perspectives. The thrill of discovery is constant — there’s always another layer to understand, another problem to crack.

Shipping is easy. Shipping the right thing, at the right time, for the right people — that’s the real game.

Whether you're here for the tech, the product talk, or just curious what a digital nomad does between commits and coffee — thanks for dropping in. DrakeWorks is my way of documenting the journey, sharing what I’ve learned, and hopefully helping a few folks along the way. And if you’re building something ambitious in the product or fintech space, I’m always open to talking shop.
